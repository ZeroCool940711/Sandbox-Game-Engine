Histogram console (activated by F11)

Key Action
F11 Toggles the console.
Esc Exits the console.
Numpad 0 Toggles graphing of luminance.
Numpad 1 Toggles graphing of the red component.
Numpad 2 Toggles graphing of the green component.
Numpad 3 Toggles graphing of the blue component.
Numpad 4 Toggles rendering of scene background.
Numpad 5 Decreases vertical range ratio.
Numpad 6 Increases vertical range ratio.
